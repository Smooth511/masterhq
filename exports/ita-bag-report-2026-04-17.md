## 📐 SIZE GUIDE FOR SCHOOL USE

For school bags, look for these minimum dimensions:
- **Height:** 35–40cm (fits A4 folders)
- **Width:** 25–30cm
- **Depth:** 10–15cm (books + supplies)

### Pin Window Sizes:
| Size | Dimensions | Approx. Pin Capacity |
|------|-----------|---------------------|
| Small | 15x15cm | ~20–30 pins |
| Medium | 20x20cm | ~40–60 pins |
| Large | 25x25cm+ | ~80+ pins |
| Double Window | 2x medium | ~100+ pins |

---

## 💡 PRO TIPS

1. **Check "A4-compatible" in listings** — Guarantees school folder size
2. **Look for "dual/double window"** — Maximum pin display
3. **Buy insert boards separately** — Pin placement is easier with a foam backing board (£3–8 on Amazon/Etsy)
4. **PU leather > canvas** for durability with heavy school loads
5. **UK-based sellers** = no customs surprise charges
6. **Check reviews for pin capacity** — Sellers often overstate window size
7. **Padded straps** — Essential for daily school use with heavy books
